Running

I like running because it's a great way to stay active and improve my physical health. It helps clear my mind and gives me better focus and energy throughout the day. I also enjoy setting distance and time goals—it’s rewarding to push myself and hit new milestones. Here's a more detailed breakdown from ChatGPT of the benefits:

  1. Good Exercise
    • Improves cardiovascular health and endurance
    • Helps maintain a healthy weight and build stamina
    • Boosts overall energy levels and physical fitness
  2. Mental Health
    • Acts as a mental reset, especially on stressful days
    • Helps me feel more present and less overwhelmed
    • Increases focus and productivity throughout the day
  3. Goal Setting
    • I enjoy setting distance or time goals and tracking my progress
    • Accomplishing goals gives a strong sense of achievement
    • Keeps me motivated with constant opportunities for improvement
